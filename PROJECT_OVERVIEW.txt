PROJECT OVERVIEW: AIRLINE CUSTOMER REVIEW ANALYTICS

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY (Non-Technical)
The Goal: To understand why passengers recommend or reject specific airlines by analyzing over 6,000 customer reviews.

What we did: We built an AI system that "reads" human reviews and translates them into data. By combining these reviews with numerical ratings (like food quality and seat comfort), we created a predictive engine that can guess if a passenger is happy with 97% accuracy.

The Top 3 Findings:
- Value for Money is King: This is the #1 driver of recommendations.
- Staff are the Backbone: Most passengers actually like the flight attendants.
- Expectation Gap: Business travelers are much harder to please than leisure travelers.

2. TECHNICAL IMPLEMENTATION (The Pillars)
Pillar 1: Data Wrangling (Pandas/NumPy)
- Handles missing values and organizes messy data.
Pillar 2: NLP - Natural Language Processing (VADER/TF-IDF)
- Converts human language into numerical "Mood Scores" and word weights.
Pillar 3: Predictive Modeling (Random Forest/XGBoost)
- The AI "Brain" that learns patterns to predict recommendations.
Pillar 4: Optimization (Tuning)
- Fine-tunes thousands of settings to get the best performance.
Pillar 5: Data Visualization
- Turns complex math into easy-to-read charts.
